Bikash, Bangladesh’s largest mobile financial service provider, announced a brand partnership with Argentina Football Association (AFA) on May 8 . They are the first and only Bangladeshi company to be the brand partner of the World Cup winning Argentina team.<\/p>\n

Bikash then announced that they will take initiatives to increase the contact of Bangladeshi fans with Argentine football stars. As part of that initiative, Bikash’s new campaign is being launched, during which shopping at Bikash will give you a chance to visit Argentina to watch the Lionel Messi-Emiliano Martinez-Julian Alvarez match.<\/p>\n

A source in the development said that the campaign, which will start from June 8, will continue till June 28. 7 people who spend the most every week using Bikash ‘Payment’ will get a total of 21 people in three weeks to go to Argentina and watch the game of Argentina national team.<\/p>\n

In this campaign, Bikash will pay the air tickets, hotel accommodation expenses of the winners. According to the FIFA international schedule, the tour will be arranged when the Argentina team plays at home next time. The winners of the campaign will be contacted by BKash and informed about the detailed itinerary including match date, opponent, venue.<\/p>\n

During the World Cup in Qatar last year, the intense love of the people of Bangladesh for the Argentina football team became the news of the global media . The strong emotions of the people of this country about Lionel Messi also touched the people of Argentina. Following this, the diplomatic relations of the two countries got a new dimension, then last month AFA’s brand partnership with Bikash.<\/p>\n

Bikash and AFA expressed hope that this partnership would allow Bangladeshi football fans to get closer to their favorite stars in the Argentina team, increase the exchange of love and strengthen the football relationship between the two countries.
